2. Falukorv Pasta Bake

Ingredients:
- FalukorvLithells 800 g
- VetemjölKungsörnen 2 kg
- Grated cheese
- Milk
- Butter
- Salt and pepper
- Pasta of your choice

Instructions:
1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
2. Slice the falukorv into rounds.
3. In a saucepan, melt butter and stir in flour to make a roux.
4. Gradually whisk in milk to make a white sauce.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Mix the cooked pasta with the white sauce and falukorv slices.
7. Transfer to a baking dish, top with grated cheese, and bake until golden and bubbly.

3. Chicken and Broccoli Stir-Fry

Ingredients:
- KycklingbröstfiléGuldfågeln Ursprung Sverige
- BroccoliICA Spanien
- Soy sauce
- Garlic
- Ginger
- Sesame oil
- Rice
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and season with salt and pepper.
2. Heat sesame oil in a pan and stir-fry the chicken until cooked through.
3. Add minced garlic and ginger to the pan.
4. Add broccoli florets and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
5. Pour in soy sauce and toss to coat.
6. Serve the chicken and broccoli stir-fry over cooked rice.
